Lobelie

Lobelia is an herb that has been traditionally used to treat respiratory conditions such as asthma, bronchitis, and coughs. It contains compounds that can help to relax the muscles in the airways, making it easier to breathe. However, it should be used with caution and under the guidance of a healthcare professional, as high doses can be toxic.


Mullein

Mullein is a herb that has been traditionally used to treat respiratory conditions such as coughs, bronchitis, and asthma. Its leaves and flowers contain compounds that can help to soothe inflammation and promote the expulsion of mucus from the respiratory tract. Additionally, mullein has been used topically in skincare products to soothe and heal the skin, particularly for conditions such as eczema and psoriasis.

Oregano

regano is a fragrant herb that is often used in cooking, but it also has medicinal properties. It is rich in antioxidants and contains compounds that have antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties, making it a popular natural remedy for various ailments, including respiratory infections, digestive problems, and skin conditions. Oregano oil is also commonly used as a natural remedy for fungal and bacterial infections, including athlete's foot and nail fungus.

Marshmello root

Marshmallow root has traditionally been used to help soothe respiratory conditions such as coughs, bronchitis, and sore throats. It contains compounds that can help to reduce inflammation in the respiratory tract, which may help to ease breathing and reduce coughing. Additionally, marshmallow root can help to soothe and protect the mucous membranes in the respiratory tract, making it a popular natural remedy for respiratory ailments.
