Aloe vera

Aloe vera is a plant with thick, fleshy leaves that contain a gel-like substance rich in vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants, which are believed to have healing and therapeutic properties for the skin and body. It has been used for thousands of years for a variety of medicinal purposes and is now widely used in cosmetics, skincare products, and alternative medicine.


Red Clover

Red clover is a herb that contains phytoestrogens, which are plant compounds that mimic the effects of estrogen in the body. This makes it a popular natural remedy for menopausal symptoms, such as hot flashes and night sweats. Additionally, red clover has been used topically in skincare products to soothe and improve the appearance of the skin, particularly for conditions such as psoriasis and eczema.

neem

Neem is a tree native to India and other parts of Southeast Asia, and its leaves, bark, and oil have been used for medicinal purposes for centuries. Neem has antimicrobial, antifungal, and anti-inflammatory properties, making it a popular natural remedy for a variety of skin conditions, including acne, psoriasis, and eczema. It is also used in hair care products for its ability to soothe the scalp and promote hair growth.

Lavender

Lavender is a fragrant herb with anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties that can soothe and calm the skin. It is often used in skincare products to help treat acne, eczema, and other skin irritations.

Rose Mary

Rosemary is a fragrant herb that has antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties, making it a popular ingredient in skincare products. It is believed to help improve circulation, reduce inflammation, and promote skin cell renewal, which can help to improve the overall health and appearance of the skin. Additionally, rosemary oil is often used in hair care products to promote hair growth and prevent dandruff.
